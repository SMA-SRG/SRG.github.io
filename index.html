Multidisciplinary Group

"The research group aims to strengthen design teams by providing the knowledge of professionals specialized in different areas. The diversity of profiles generates a multidisciplinary environment that increases the ability to respond to current design and construction problems. The group is also in charge of doing research that generates new concepts and technologies for their implementation in projects. This allows us to be at the forefront of architecture and to make innovative and efficient proposals."
